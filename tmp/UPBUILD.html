About

This small architectural bureau was founded by two former colleagues and friends, Lesley Prinkle and Reginald Matthews.

 

At that moment they both worked at the SDK agency, but both of them felt unsatisfied with how the big business does operate and how both the quality and client’s best interests suffer from that.

So they’ve decided to found some small venture of their own, moving to LA and hoping for the best.

Just half a year ago, with the addition of a part-time engineer onboard, the team was finally able to take on even more complex projects!

Keep in mind, that if you’ll need any building designed by these guys, it won’t be cheap! It will be reasonably cheap!
